Ideation

  • info_outlineWhat is the name of your app?

    Guess Show!

  • perm_identityWhat collaborative activity can people do together using your app?

    Players take turns to draw something with a tip of number of characters. The rest players take turns to guess the word based on what that person draw.

  • group_workWhat is the ideal number of concurrent users (must be at least 10 but no more than 40)?

    Ten People

  • credit_cardWhat skills do people need in order to do this activity?

    Basic drawing skill and good expression and understanding skill.

  • storeIs this activity for fun or for something practical?

    This activity is mainly for killing time.

  • webHow can a person see what everyone else is doing?

    A player can see what others are talking in a chatting area.

  • subtitlesHow can a person see the most recent result of everyone’s collaborative effort?

    A player can see the history in the chatting area.

  • gamesWhat can an admin see (i.e., God’s mode)?

    All the login users.
